Yet Again

March 2026 · 3 minute read

Things wouldn’t just let me live in peace

As you might’ve known already, my mind has been a cluttered mess since God knows when. A lot has been bothering me and I’m tired of it.

People, family even, makes me lose hope of what morals are supposed to be. They tell me to not get pressured while putting the name as to start a legacy, or to end a bad one, somehow. Prioritizing what people would feel when I finally commit suicide than making sure that I can think right and pull shit up from the root. It makes me feel stupid, surrounded by people with twisted morals seeing themselves as saviors. Hell, I can assure myself anytime I could do even better for myself.

What I hate about this thinking is that I feel pressured to just take it all without remorse, or else I’ll be ungrateful of them being there for me materially, since that’s their sole responsibility anyway.

Well, regardless of how I feel about my environment, everything just kept nudging me. I couldn’t handle it as effectively as before. I felt like I had no one to rely and lay my head on. I’m in a pace where no one exists, not even me. I always looked back in the past, and looked to the future, well, less, but the point I’m emphasizing is that I didn’t feel grounded anymore like I always am when a pal needs someone.

I only wanted to have somebody like me in my life that tries everything I can to look inside. Hopelessness is the only thing this shithole had managed to give me, excuses to let things go for good. So I attempted suicide again. I took several days worth of medicines in attempt to overdose, and all I received is nothing, not even holistic care, the only thing I was looking for when I was at my lowest.

All the world does is disappoint me instead of helping me to stand on my own feet. Everything I do seem to be just for others now. I don’t even know how to take care of myself anymore. God forbid something like this makes an excuse for it to happen again. I feel hopeless about the people around me, yet they all remind me of only themselves. I don’t give a shit if one feels sad for themself anymore if something happens to me, for fuck’s sake I’m already dead, and you’re still making me understand others as priority when I’m the one who’s struggling to walk on my own like a goddamn toddler.

I don’t get what’s wrong with everything, and everything still makes me wonder if I should keep on moving blindfolded or if I should just let things go in two different ways.
